Frequently Asked Questions

1 What areas do you serve?
We currently serve united kingdom and its enviro and surrounding neighborhoods. If you’re unsure whether we cover your location, feel free to reach out!
2 Do I need to be home during the cleaning?
Not at all. Many of our clients prefer to leave a key or give access instructions. Your home is in safe hands with our trusted, background-checked professionals.
3 What’s included in a standard cleaning?
standard cleaning includes dusting, vacuuming, mopping, kitchen and bathroom wipe-downs, trash removal, and more. Deep cleaning or special requests can be added as extras.
4 How do I pay for your services?
We accept cash, bank transfers, and mobile payment options. All payments are due upon completion unless otherwise arranged.
5 Can I schedule recurring cleanings?
Absolutely! We offer flexible weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly packages to keep your space consistently fresh.
