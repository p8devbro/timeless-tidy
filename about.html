About Our Company

workExperience

At Timeless Tidy, we do more than just clean — we care for your home as if it were our own. With a trusted team of trained professionals and a reputation built on reliability, we deliver top-notch residential and commercial cleaning services tailored to your specific needs.

Whether it’s a one-time deep clean, weekly upkeep, or post-event restoration, Timeless Tidy ensures your space is sparkling, stress-free, and ready to impress.

Company Story

Learn About Our Journey

Now

01 Dec 2024

Serving Homes & Offices Nationwide

By the end of 2024, Timeless Tidy had grown into a trusted name across the UK. From London flats to Manchester offices, we deliver reliable, professional cleaning with a personal touch. Our mission remains: spotless results, every time.

15 Sept 2024

Launched Our Web App

We launched our full-featured web app, allowing clients to book, manage, and track cleanings with ease. This digital upgrade streamlined operations, reduced response times, and improved the customer experience across the board.

15 Sept 2024

20 Jul 2024

Expanded Service Offerings

Beyond standard home cleaning, we introduced office cleaning, end-of-tenancy deep cleans, Airbnb turnovers, and eco-friendly options. Listening to our customers helped us grow smarter and serve better.

10 Apr 2024

Built Our First Core Team

We hired our first five full-time cleaners and two admin staff. Every team member went through our training program, ensuring quality, consistency, and trust from day one.

10 Apr 2024

14 Feb 2024

First 100 Cleanings Completed

Word-of-mouth took off quickly. Within just six weeks, we completed our first 100 cleanings with glowing reviews. We knew we were on to something special.

Launch

Launched Timeless Tidy on 01 Jan 2024

Founded in London, Timeless Tidy was born from the belief that cleaning should be simple, stress-free, and consistent. With backgrounds in hospitality and tech, our founders set out to build a modern, customer-first cleaning service powered by smart systems and caring people.
